Privacy Policy for Voxtalk

Last updated: December 2023

Voxtalk ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Voxtalk mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Account Information: Phone number, email address, display name, profile picture
  • Contact Information: Contacts from your device (with your permission) to enable calling and messaging
  • Communication Data: Call history, chat messages, and media shared within the app

Technical Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: App features used, call duration, frequency of use
  • Log Information: Crash reports, performance data, error logs

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and maintain Voxtalk services
  • Enable end-to-end encrypted calling and messaging
  • Sync your contacts for communication purposes
  • Send notifications about calls and messages
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Ensure security and prevent fraud
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. End-to-End Encryption

All voice calls, video calls, and messages are protected with end-to-end encryption. This means:

  • Only you and the people you communicate with can read or listen to what is sent
  • We cannot access your call content or message content
  • Encryption keys are stored only on your device

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase: For authentication, cloud storage, and crash reporting
  • Agora: For voice and video calling infrastructure
  • Google Play Services: For app distribution and updates

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information only:

  • With your consent
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect our rights and safety
  • With service providers who assist our operations (under strict confidentiality)

6. Data Retention

  • Account Information: Retained until you delete your account
  • Call History: Stored locally on your device
  • Messages: End-to-end encrypted and stored temporarily for delivery
  • Technical Data: Aggregated and anonymized for analytics

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and update your personal information
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Export your data
  • Opt-out of non-essential communications
  • Withdraw consent for contact synchronization

8. Children's Privacy

Voxtalk is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

9. Security Measures

We implement appropriate security measures including:

  • End-to-end encryption for all communications
  • Secure data transmission using TLS
  • Regular security assessments
  • Access controls and authentication

10. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed on servers located outside your country, but we ensure adequate protection through standard contractual clauses.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or via email.
